Collaborative Learning Flow

A simple view of how teams learn together inside the student portal.

Start

Student logs into the portal.

Process

Student selects a theme or subject for the session.

Process

System assigns students into diverse teams based on region and background.

Process

Each team receives a problem statement or challenge.

Process

Students work on the problem within a defined time interval.

Discussion

Team discussion phase where each student shares ideas and approaches.

Process

Team evaluates ideas and understands different perspectives.

Decision

Is the team satisfied with the solution?

Yes: move to final submission. No: return to discussion and refinement.
Submission

Final solution submission by the team.

Reflection

Reflection or feedback stage to capture learning from the activity.

End

Cycle completes. Student is ready for the next challenge.
